About GPMO

We make competition math exciting, welcoming, and seriously rewarding for students who love a challenge.

Our Story

GPMO started with one big idea: high-level math should feel exciting, not intimidating. We create contests and resources that help students grow from curious learners into confident problem solvers.

Inspired by the gentoo penguin, we value resilience, teamwork, and momentum. Our community is full of students who push each other, share strategies, and celebrate every breakthrough.

Our goal is simple: help the next generation of thinkers build skill, confidence, and joy through creative mathematical challenge.

Our Mission

Challenge students with meaningful problems and help them build sharp reasoning, grit, and creativity.

Our Values

We run on excellence, integrity, collaboration, and inclusivity so every student feels supported and seen.

Our Vision

A future where every student can discover their math potential and chase problems they are proud to solve.

What We Offer

  • Annual Competitions

    Multiple competition levels designed for different age groups and skill levels, ensuring everyone can participate and feel challenged.

  • Practice Resources

    Access to past tests, detailed solutions, and practice problems to help students prepare and improve their skills.

  • Recognition and Awards

    Top performers receive certificates, awards, and recognition for their outstanding mathematical achievements.

  • Community Support

    Connect with fellow math enthusiasts, share strategies, and learn from each other in a supportive community environment.

Our Team

GPMO is powered by a crew of passionate problem writers, competitors, and mentors. Our team includes mathkiddus, Inferno2332, Kempu33334, NamelyOrange, and lele0305, who work together to create engaging competitions and resources for our community.
